================================================================================
📝 DATABASE MATCHING ALGORITHM
================================================================================

Match Percentage = (Student Average Score / Major Cut-off) × 100

Classification:
  90-100% → Rất Phù Hợp (Very Suitable)
  80-90%  → Phù Hợp (Suitable)
  70-80%  → Khá Phù Hợp (Quite Suitable)
  60-70%  → Bình Thường (Average)
  <60%    → Ít Phù Hợp (Less Suitable)
